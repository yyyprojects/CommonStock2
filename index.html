What is Common Stock?

Think of it as buying a small piece of a company. It's a share of ownership that gives you a claim on the company's future success and a say in how it's run.

How You Make Money

Returns come from two sources: the stock's price increasing (capital gains) and the company sharing profits (dividends).

Growth vs. Income

Growth stocks (like tech startups) reinvest profits to grow fast. Your main return comes from the stock price soaring.

Income stocks (like established utility companies) are mature businesses that pay out regular dividends, providing a steady income stream.

The Ultimate Risk: Liquidation Priority
